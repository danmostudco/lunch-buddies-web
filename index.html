Lunch Buddies

What is lunch buddies?

This app helps build relationships between members of a growing team. Each week, users will have the opportunity to join a small group of teammates for lunch. The @lunch_buddies_bot takes care of all the logistics.

How does it work?

Installation

Install the app using the button at the top of this page, or from the Slack App Directory.

Basic Usage

Step 1: Invite your teammates to the "#lunch_buddies" channel

The app works by polling the members of a Slack channel to see who is interested in participating. When you installed the app, a default "#lunch_buddies" channel was created. You should invite your teammates to this channel.

Advanced Usage

It is possible to have multiple polls going at the same time; they are uniquely defined by the channel from which they were created. For example, you could "create" a poll in the #seattle_office channel and also in the #san_francisco_office channel. Members of each channel will be independently polled and grouped.

Privacy policy

This app will create a channel called "lunch-buddies" and will poll each member of that channel whenever the "/lunch_buddies_create" command is run. When the "/lunch_buddies_close" command is run, it will start a private group message with each group. The only information saved is the answers to the interactive question about participating.
